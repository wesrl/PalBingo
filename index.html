How to Play

The objective is to get a "Bingo" as quickly as possible by completing the goals in five squares in a line horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

  • Items: Goals written as just Item names (such as "Ingot" or "Cooked Berries") require you to get that item and have it in your Inventory or base at the end of the game. If you lose the item by completing the Bingo by dying (e.g. "Reach Max Crime Status and spawn level 50 Guards before dying" as the last goal) you do not need to recollect the item.
  • Actions: If the goal describes an action (for example "Capture a rideable mount"), the goal is completed once you capture the mount. It doesn't matter what happens to the mount after.
  • Builds: For goals which ask you to place something in the world (like "Build a 2x2x2 Base"), you must make sure that the build does not get destroyed for the duration of the Bingo. You don't have to show the build at the end, so if you didn't see it get destroyed it counts.
  • Locations: Goals which list a location or screenshot that you need to visit. A screenshot is the best way to log you have been to a location.

Tip: You can click on the squares to change their colour which is useful for planning or to mark off completed goals. Use the colour slider in the Options menu to choose how many colours you want to use. You can also hover over the square and press (1-6) to select a colour or (0/q) to remove the colour! Distinct symbols on the squares for each colour can be toggled in the Options menu.

Tip: Hover over a "?" to get more information about that goal.

Creating the World

Create a new world in Palworld. Make a unique character. Load the world so that it can generate but don't move until you're ready. Pause the game to make sure time of day doesn't change or you get attacked. When you're ready, reveal the sheet if it's hidden and start playing!

Note: Third-party applications or mods are up to you. They will change the difficulty depending on what you select

Playing with Others

If you're racing together with other players, generate a Bingo sheet and then link it to them so that everyone has the same goals. You can hide the table in the Options menu before generating the seed so that everyone can reveal the sheet when you start. Make sure everyone uses the same Seed for the world too!

Other

These rules (and goal descriptions) give you an idea of what you can do, however if you play alone or everyone in the race agrees, then use whatever rules you want.
